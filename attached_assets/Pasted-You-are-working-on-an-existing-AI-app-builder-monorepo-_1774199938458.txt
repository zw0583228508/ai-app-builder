PHASE 4 — FIX THE STREAMING / WRITING FLOW
============================================================

Target:
artifacts/app-builder/src/hooks/use-chat-stream.ts
and adjust artifacts/app-builder/src/components/ChatPanel.tsx as needed

Current problem:
The assistant’s writing flow feels stuck, then suddenly writes a whole line or big chunk at once.
The stream does not feel fluid or natural.
It feels laggy and unpleasant.

This must be fixed.

Likely cause:
There is an artificial frontend typewriter/buffering layer on top of SSE streaming that is making the stream feel delayed and bursty.

Required:
- make normal text streaming appear continuously and smoothly
- avoid “freeze then dump” behavior
- avoid aggressive buffering
- avoid abrupt final flushes
- prioritize low-latency live rendering
- if necessary, reduce or remove the artificial typewriter effect for normal chat text
- preserve code preview streaming behavior
- keep the experience responsive during long answers

The chat response must feel:
- immediate
- continuous
- natural
- premium
- alive

Streaming UX rules:
- the user should feel the response is arriving live
- no obvious stalls
- no big jump at the end
- no artificial lag
- no visibly delayed queue draining

Also review:
- scroll behavior during streaming
- excessive rerenders
- unnecessary animated indicators that amplify jank
